## Overview

**Zendesk** is a flexible customer support and service management platform designed to help teams communicate efficiently with clients across multiple channels. It centralizes conversations, tickets, and user interactions into a single structured environment, making support processes clear and manageable.

The platform focuses on clarity and speed, allowing support agents to respond faster while maintaining consistent service quality. With an interface built around workflows and automation, **Zendesk** adapts easily to growing teams and evolving customer needs.

Its scalable architecture supports businesses of different sizes, enabling smooth handling of support requests while maintaining visibility into performance and response metrics.
